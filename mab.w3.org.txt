  <section>
    <h2>What is Web4?</h2>
    <p>Web4 is the next evolutionary stage of the internet beyond Web3. While Web3 focuses on decentralization, blockchain, and user ownership, Web4 is all about hyper-connected intelligence, seamless integration of AI, IoT, and immersive experiences, and human-centered digital ecosystems. Think of it as the internet becoming smart, adaptive, and deeply personal.</p>
  </section>

  <!-- Key Principles -->
  <section>
    <h2>Key Principles of Web4</h2>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Intelligent Web:</strong> AI and machine learning are embedded natively into the web, enabling smart recommendations, automation, and predictive services. Every website, app, and service can adapt in real-time to user behavior.</li>
      <li><strong>Decentralization Evolved:</strong> Beyond blockchain: distributed computing, peer-to-peer networks, and decentralized AI systems reduce reliance on centralized servers.</li>
      <li><strong>Immersive & Multi-Sensory:</strong> Full integration with AR/VR/MR for mixed reality experiences. Users can interact with digital content as if it’s part of the physical world.</li>
      <li><strong>Interoperability & Universal Access:</strong> Apps and data seamlessly work across devices, platforms, and ecosystems. Data portability is standard: your digital identity, assets, and preferences move with you securely.</li>
      <li><strong>Human-Centric Design:</strong> Privacy, ethics, and sustainability are baked in. AI respects consent, transparency, and fairness.</li>
      <li><strong>Hyper-Personalization:</strong> Web4 understands context, emotion, and environment. Personalized experiences, recommendations, and interfaces are dynamic and real-time.</li>
      <li><strong>Edge & IoT Integration:</strong> Internet of Things + Edge Computing = real-time, local intelligence. Your devices communicate and make decisions without always needing a central server.</li>
    </ul>
  </section>
